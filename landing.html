About me
I'm Hana and I'm a

A literary major, journalist and writer with an affinity for all things tech. Earned my bachelor degree in English Literature in my home country Lebanon and worked as a journalist for over 10 years between Beirut and Dubai. Over the course of my career which was mainly print and later on extended into digital, I was very interested in how information was curated and distributed. I often found myself drawn to the technical side of things, learning html and working with different CMS platforms. Developing those skills came in handy but remained very curious about what lays under the hood. After my long journalistic stint,I found my way back to Academia in Belgium where I joined KU Leuven for a Masters in Western Literature. After finalizing my degree, I joined the Advanced Master Program of Digital Humanities program. This program has opened up an entire new universe of possibilities and rekindled my curiosity and my love for digital and all things tech. From scripting languages and web development, to data visualizations and AI, everyday I am introduced to new ways I can become a better humanities graduate, journalist and writer. In the digital age we live in, both code and data have become the new gold. While the future direction of this blog is still very much up in the air, I thank you for taking the time to browse those pages and I invite you to come back in the future to see my progress. It is my intention with this blog to encourage my readers to explore new passions and creative avenues no matter how complicated it might seem.
